Welcome to Semvis Air


With this website we have set ourselves the goal of visualize and publish air quality data of Münster, to make them reasonable to its citizens. Citizens should have the opportunity to view environmental information in their personal living space and thus may also weigh risk potential for health.

The realization of this project is largely supported by the development of the "Air Quality Eggs". The "Air Quality Egg" is a small egg-shaped sensor platform which shall allow each person to measure the air quality in his living space and to share them with others. To get a further insight in the development, we provide you with a video on the right side, made by the developers. However, this is only available in English. If you already own an "Air Quality Egg" and want to share it, please klick on "Help". There you will find an explanation.
